# Prompt-Based Datasets for Video Quality Assessment

This repository provides the three prompt-based datasets (PBDs) associated with the research paper **"Towards the Development of QoE Generative Models"**. These datasets were specifically designed to fine-tune a BERT architecture for Quality of Experience (QoE) prediction within the domain of Video Quality Assessment (VQA). 

Additionally, we include the comprehensive empirical results obtained across three distinct test scenarios. These results highlight key statistical performance metrics—namely **MAE**, **RMSE**, **PLCC**, and **SRCC**—alongside their corresponding graphical evaluations, including **scatter plots** and **error distribution histograms**.

## Repository Structure

The repository includes the following files:

### Prompt-Based Datasets
* `PBD1.xlsx`: The dataset containing 81 instances, generated from the source raw video data `RVD1`. Used for training and baseline validation.
* `PBD2.xlsx`: The dataset containing 561 instances, generated by processing the raw video data `RVD1` and `RVD2`. Used for training and cross-dataset validation.
* `PBD3.xlsx`: The comprehensive heterogeneous dataset comprising 388 curated samples (~40% of total data) from multiple studies (`RVD1`, `RVD2` and `RVD3`), optimized to maximize technical diversity during fine-tuning.

### Experimental Results
* `results_test_scenario_1.xlsx`: Simulation results for Test Scenario 1 (Baseline validation), showing strong positive correlation with subjective MOS.
* `results_test_scenario_2.xlsx`: Simulation results for Test Scenario 2 (Generalization test on unfamiliar features like QP), demonstrating the performance boundary of out-of-domain technical parameters.
* `results_test_scenario_3.xlsx`: Simulation results for Test Scenario 3, demonstrating how a balanced variety of heterogeneous data restores predictive accuracy across unseen prompts.
